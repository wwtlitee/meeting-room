# Internet Automation Monetization Panel

Use this preset when the user wants to explore legal internet automation monetization, AI-assisted online businesses, automated lead generation, content systems, marketplaces, affiliate operations, micro-SaaS, data products, or workflow automation services.

## Goal

Find practical, legal, low-to-medium risk internet business ideas that can be validated quickly, automated responsibly, and measured with clear revenue assumptions.

## Mandatory Boundaries

Reject or redesign ideas involving:

- spam, deceptive outreach, fake reviews, fake engagement, or account farming
- credential abuse, scraping private/personal data, bypassing access controls, or evading rate limits
- financial promises, medical/legal claims, or regulated advice without proper qualification
- copyright laundering, plagiarism, impersonation, or undisclosed synthetic media
- platform terms violations that create account, legal, or reputational risk

## Discussion Format

1. Moderator states assumptions and asks for missing constraints if necessary.
2. Product and market roles propose candidate models.
3. Engineering and automation roles sketch feasible workflows.
4. Growth and sales roles evaluate distribution.
5. Finance role models rough economics.
6. Compliance and reality roles veto or redesign risky ideas.
7. Moderator ranks Top 3 and gives a validation plan.
